Leading Across Borders and Time Zones

Develop the cultural intelligence and remote leadership skills to manage global teams with confidence, clarity and connection.

⏱ 90 mins estimated 📋 5 Lessons 🌍 Global Teams Focus

By the end of this module you will be able to…

    >Identify key cultural dimensions that affect global team dynamics >Adapt your communication style for different cultural contexts >Apply strategies to build trust across remote and distributed teams >Manage time zone challenges and asynchronous collaboration effectively >Navigate conflict and misunderstanding in cross-cultural settings >Lead with cultural intelligence and inclusive decision-making

Module Lessons

01

Understanding Cultural Dimensions

Explore key frameworks such as Erin Meyer's Culture Map to understand how culture shapes communication, hierarchy, trust and decision-making in the workplace.

⏱ 20 mins
02

Communicating Across Cultures

Learn how to adjust your communication style — from direct to indirect, formal to informal — to build rapport and avoid misinterpretation with global colleagues.

⏱ 20 mins
03

Building Trust in Remote and Global Teams

Discover practical strategies for building psychological safety, connection and accountability when your team is spread across countries and time zones.

⏱ 15 mins
04

Managing Time Zones and Async Collaboration

Master the tools and habits that keep global teams aligned — from scheduling with empathy to structuring async updates that respect everyone's working hours.

⏱ 15 mins
05

Leading with Cultural Intelligence

Bring it all together — develop your personal CQ (Cultural Quotient), practice inclusive leadership and create a team culture where difference is a strength.

⏱ 20 mins
