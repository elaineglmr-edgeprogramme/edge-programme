Leading Across Borders and Time Zones

Develop the cultural intelligence and remote leadership skills to manage global teams with confidence, clarity and connection.

⏱ 90 mins estimated 📋 6 Lessons 🌍 Global Teams Focus

By the end of this module you will be able to…

    >Understand the realities of leading global and hybrid teams today >Establish effective team norms and rhythms for distributed working >Support team performance, wellbeing and conflict resolution at a distance >Manage time zones, workload and team rhythm fairly and effectively >Lead performance and wellbeing in a remote or hybrid context >Identify and address communication breakdowns and cultural misunderstandings

Module Lessons

01

Global and Hybrid Team Realities

Explore the landscape of modern global and hybrid teams — the opportunities they bring and the unique challenges leaders face when their teams span borders and time zones.

⏱ 15 mins
02

Establishing Effective Team Norms and Rhythms

Learn how to co-create clear team agreements, communication norms and working rhythms that keep distributed teams aligned, accountable and connected.

⏱ 15 mins
03

Driving Performance, Wellbeing, and Conflict Resolution at a Distance

Discover strategies for maintaining high performance and team wellbeing remotely, and develop the skills to identify and resolve conflict before it escalates.

⏱ 15 mins
04

Time Zones, Rhythm and Workload Management

Master practical approaches to scheduling across time zones with fairness and empathy, managing workload distribution and building sustainable team rhythms.

⏱ 15 mins
05

Leading Performance and Wellbeing Remotely

Build your confidence as a remote leader — setting clear expectations, running effective check-ins and creating a culture of trust, visibility and psychological safety.

⏱ 15 mins
06

Communication Breakdowns and Cultural Misunderstandings

Develop cultural intelligence to recognise, prevent and recover from communication breakdowns — and lead with greater sensitivity, clarity and inclusion across cultures.

⏱ 15 mins

Reflection Prompt

Think about your current or most recent team. How well do your team norms support remote or hybrid working? What is one change you could make to better support performance, wellbeing or inclusion across borders?

Practice Activity

    >Map your team: List where each team member is based and their time zone. >Audit your norms: Do you have clear agreements about communication, availability and meetings? If not, draft one norm you could introduce. >Identify a gap
